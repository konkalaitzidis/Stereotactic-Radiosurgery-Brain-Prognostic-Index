Predicting one-year survival

One-year survival predictions can be obtained for any patient by using the coefficients and mean values presented in Table 2 of the manuscript and the equation:

\(\hat{s}(1) = s_0 \cdot (1)^{e^{\left(\sum_{i=1}^{p} \beta_i (x_i - \bar{x}_i)\right)}}\)

for variables \(i = 1, . . . , p. S0(1)\) denotes the baseline survival at one year and \(x Ì…i\) is the mean value for variable \(i\).
